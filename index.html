About Me

Welcome to Feng Shui Destiny Studio

My affinity with Chinese metaphysics started when I was in middle school. Back then, I wrote diaries for a few years, and noticed a certain patterns kept appeared in specific months related to my emotions, thinking, ideas, and my inner intuition ran high. One afternoon in Biology class, I daydreamed and the light bulb in my mind went off – the law of nature. We all get impacted by the energy of the four seasons – Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter – which translates back to the law of nature and the 5 elements. I had never set out my heart to become a metaphysics consultant, but I was always very intrigued by this subject.

Fast forward, I led a normal life. Graduated high school. Graduated university with French Studies, and Accounting studies. I've worked in the private sector for several years. Eventually all the stars started to line up before I knew it. Life pulled me towards becoming a consultant, providing guidance or solution to individuals who need help. Then, I ventured myself out there working solo, independently in the world of metaphysics.
